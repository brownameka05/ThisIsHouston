HOW WE CAN HELP

HERE FOR YOU

We have created THIS IS HOUSTON for our LGBTQ+ community and their families, to provide the resources and help needed for a stronger base. We envision a healthier society marked by permanent, positive changes in attitudes and behaviors toward the LGBT communities, and the ability of all LGBT individuals to realize their fullest potential.
